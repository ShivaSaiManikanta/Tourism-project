GOA

WHY YOU SHOULD VISIT GOA??

It’s no secret that Goa is a favourite beach destination in India. It has a laid-back lifestyle, hippie vibe, glorious scenic beaches, natural tranquillity, beach sports, and awesome parties. It’s a great destination year-round, but visiting Goa during summer is quite a special experience.

 >Dudhsagar Falls
 >Augada Fort
 >Bondla Wildlife Sanctuary
 >Baga Beach
 >Goa chitra museum
